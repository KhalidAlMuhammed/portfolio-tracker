1-Month Stock Performance

Performance Analysis

This chart displays the 1-month performance of each stock in your portfolio. The percentage change indicates how much each stock has gained or lost over the past month.

Interpreting the results:

  • Positive values (green) indicate gains, while negative values (red) indicate losses.
  • Compare individual stock performance to overall market trends.
  • Consider how each stock's performance contributes to your portfolio's overall return.
  • Use this information to make informed decisions about rebalancing or adjusting your holdings.
